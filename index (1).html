Why we reconfirm

When a guest books a hotel through TravClan, the booking goes through multiple systems — our portal, the agent, and the hotel's own reservation system. By the time it lands on the hotel's end, things can go wrong. Room types get mismatched. Meal plans don't show up. Some bookings don't even make it into the hotel's system at all.

The guest doesn't know any of this. They show up at the hotel expecting a smooth check-in.

"Reconfirmation is how we make sure that never happens on our watch. We call the hotel before the guest travels, verify every detail, and fix any issues while we still have time."

Why timing matters

Once the guest is standing at the front desk with a suitcase, it's too late — the cost to fix it goes up, the guest experience goes down, and TravClan takes the blame. This is not a back-office task. It is the last checkpoint between a booking on a screen and a guest having a good experience.

How to build your queue each morning

  • Sort by check-in date first — P1 always at the top, no exceptions
  • Within the same tier, Volt/Nikola agent bookings go before others
  • Within same tier + same agent, earlier bookings first
  • Check email replies and AI bot log before you start calling — confirmed bookings are removed from your queue
Call script & steps
1

Opening — get the right person

"Hello, good [morning/afternoon/evening]! Am I speaking with the Reservations or Front Desk team at [Hotel Name]?"

Always ask for Reservations first. Once connected, introduce yourself:

"Thank you. My name is [Your Name], calling from TravClan. I'd like to reconfirm a booking at your property. The guest name is [Guest Name]. Check-in date is [Date]. Check-out date is [Date]. Could you please confirm if this reservation is active in your system?"
2

Verification checklist — go through every point

  • Check-in and check-out dates — confirm both match exactly
  • Number of rooms
  • Room type + inclusions (sea view, pool access, club access etc.)
  • Number of guests (pax) per room and total
  • Guest names per room
  • Meal plan — ask them to read it back, don't accept vague confirmation
  • Payment — confirm booking is prepaid and hotel has received VCC details
  • Additional charges at check-in (city tax, resort fee, deposit) — note amount + currency
  • Hotel Confirmation Number (HCN) + name and department of person confirming
3

Closing the call

If everything checks out

Update system with HCN, name, department. Close warmly: "Thank you so much for your time and assistance, [Name]. We really appreciate your help. Have a lovely day. Goodbye!"

If there's a discrepancy

Note it, update status, escalate. Do not try to resolve it yourself unless it's a trivial name spelling. "Thank you for letting us know. We will look into this from our end and follow up with you shortly."

If hotel cannot find the booking

Try once more with guest name + date spelled out. If still not found: "Alright, thank you for checking. We will look into this from our end and may call back." Then hang up and escalate immediately.
